WWE Live Event Results From Augusta, Georgia (5/13/23)

Superstars from both WWE RAW & SmackDown are settling into their new brands, and a mixture of superstars were seen in Augusta, GA over the weekend for the latest live event. Check out the results from the show below courtesy of Wrestling Bodyslam.

  • Bianca Belair defeated Asuka
  • Omos defeated Dolph Ziggler
  • AJ Styles defeated Austin Theory by DQ after Theory was caught hitting a low blow on Styles.
  • Sami Zayn & Kevin Owens defeated The Street Profits ad Ludwig Kaiser & Giovanni Vinci
  • Rhea Ripley defeated Tegan Nox
  • Gunther defeated Johnny Gargano
  • Cody Rhodes defeated Finn Balor
