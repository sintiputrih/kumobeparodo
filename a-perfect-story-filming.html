A Perfect Story: Unveiling the Filming Locations

Netflix’s “A Perfect Story” is a Spanish romantic drama series based on the same novel by Elsabet Benavent. It follows Margot, the heiress of a significant hotel empire, and David, who works three jobs to make ends meet and sleeps on the couch of his best friends. Despite having quite different lives, Margot and David are united by their shared faith. The two realise they have more in common than they initially believed as they get to know one another. In the end, Margot and David cooperate with one another and learn that they are the only ones who can assist one other in reuniting with their respective loving partners.

The romantic comedy, which was originally titled “Un Cuento Perfecto,” stars a cast of excellent actors, including Anna Castillo, Lvaro Mel, Ingrid Garca Jonsson, Ane Gabarain, Elena Irureta, and Tai Fati. The audience are sure to be interested in learning where “A Perfect Story” was actually filmed given the utilisation of various fascinating places throughout each episode, including the lovely Greek landscapes. If your mind is pondering the same things, let’s finally put an end to your curiosity!

A Perfect Story Filming Locations

In particular, Mykonos and Madrid are used to film “A Perfect Story” in Greece and Spain. According to sources, the first season of the love series’ main photography began in October 2022 and ended in January 2023. Let’s go right along and explore every single one of the picturesque locales that are featured in the Netflix film without wasting any time!

Mykonos, Greece

The majority of the crucial scenes for “A Perfect Story” were filmed on the Cyclades-region island of Mykonos in Greece. The production crew moves around the island to shoot various scenes, both indoors and outside, against appropriate backdrops. For instance, they made camp on Agios Sostis, one of the island’s elegant, secluded, sandy beaches, which is also one of its quietest.

Mykonos, also referred to as The Island of the Winds, is well renowned for its exciting nightlife and for welcoming homosexual travellers. These facets of the island may be discernible in the film. The Mykonos windmills, Little Venice, the Armenistis Lighthouse, the Church of Paraportiani, and Elia Beach are a few additional well-known monuments and locations that are likely to be visible in the background of various scenarios.

Madrid, Spain

In the later phases of filming, the “A Perfect Story” filmmaking crew also travelled to Madrid, the capital of Spain, for shooting purposes. You might be able to recognise some well-known structures and sites in the background, like the Plaza Mayor, Cibeles Palace, the Gran Via, the Puerta de Alcalá, and the Royal Palace of Madrid, as they film numerous sequences on location throughout different neighbourhoods and streets of Madrid. Other films that have used the city as a backdrop include “A Perfect Story,” “Room in Rome,” “Through My Window,” “All About My Mother,” “The Cook of Castamar,” and “In Love All Over Again.”
