Backstage Update on Christians In-Ring Return &amp; WWE Future, Bo Dallas Comments on Bray Wyatts

Backstage Update on Christian's In-Ring Return & WWE Future

According to F4WOnline.com, while it was originally believed Christian would receive medical clearance to return to the ring in the Andre Battle Royal last night, Christian has NOT in fact received medical clearance to return from a concussion, and many in WWE are beginning to question his in-ring future.

Bo Dallas Comments on Bray Wyatt's WWE Success

TBO.com recently interviewed WWE NXT star Bo Dallas, and he had the following to say regarding his brother Windham Rotunda, aka Bray Wyatt:

"I'm very happy for my brother. He's doing very well for himself. He's developed this character and actually became him. It's a crazy process watching."
