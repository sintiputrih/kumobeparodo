Where did Jayden Daniels go to high school?

By Fan Arch February 15, 2024 21:57

Photo by AP Photo/Vasha Hunt, File

Jayden Daniels, the renowned American football quarterback, attended Cajon High School in San Bernardino, California. Here's a detailed breakdown of his high school journey and achievements.

High School Experience

Jayden Daniels, born on December 18, 2000, attended Cajon High School, a renowned educational institution in San Bernardino, California. He made a significant impact during his time at Cajon High School and was widely recognized as a standout athlete.

Recruiting Profile

According to 247Sports, Jayden Daniels was an exceptional talent in high school, being ranked as the No. 1 dual-threat quarterback in the country, the No. 5 recruit in California, and the No. 35 overall recruit in the Class of 2019. His impressive skills and performance on the field garnered attention from numerous Division I college football programs, leading to 25 offers from esteemed institutions.

College Commitment

Despite receiving offers from various reputable schools, Jayden Daniels ultimately committed to Arizona State University. His commitment to Arizona State was solidified in December 2018, and he officially signed with the university shortly after on Early Signing Day. This commitment marked the beginning of his collegiate football journey, where he continued to excel as a talented quarterback.

Impact at Arizona State

Upon enrolling early at Arizona State in January, Jayden Daniels made an immediate impact during his true freshman season. He displayed remarkable skills on the field, completing 205 of 338 pass attempts for 2,943 passing yards and 17 touchdowns through the air. Additionally, he showcased his versatility by contributing 355 rushing yards on the ground and three rushing touchdowns.

Transition to LSU

After his successful stint at Arizona State, Jayden Daniels transferred to Louisiana State University (LSU), where he continued to make waves in collegiate football. His transition to LSU marked a new chapter in his career, and he further solidified his reputation as a top-level quarterback.

Jayden Daniels' high school journey at Cajon High School in San Bernardino, California laid the foundation for his remarkable achievements in collegiate football, leading to a successful career as a standout quarterback at both Arizona State University and Louisiana State University.
