Did Joe Burrow Get ACL Surgery? Current Health Update

The 25-year-old, Joe Burrow underwent surgery to repair torn ACL and MCL in December 2020.

Joe, in 2020 tore his ACL and MCL in his left knee, and also some damage was done to his PCL and meniscus.

In the second half of week 11, of a game against the Washington Football Team, Burrow was hit while throwing a pass.

“The surgery was successful and  Joe is expected to make a complete recovery.” the team said in a press release.

Burrow threw for 244 yards, plus two touchdowns in a 26-19 victory, bringing the Bengals to their first playoff win since 1991 in a wildcard round.
