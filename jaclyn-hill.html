    Personal Trainer

    Jaclyn works out regularly to keep herself fit and to keep her toned figure in the best shape. Overall, she hits the gym 4 to 5 times a week.

    To complement her efforts in the gym, she follows a healthy and balanced diet. She tries to refrain from any form of sugar consumption and has often spoken about the difficulties in dealing with sugar cravings in her social media feed.

    She also abstains from dairy products, consumes fewer carbs, and alcohol.

    To make sure that she sticks to her healthy diet while traveling or working away from home, she packs her food and carries it along.

    Jaclyn Hill Facts

  • During her school days, she was a cheerleader and by the age of 13, she had become a cheer coach for the Wildcats.
  • She got braces at the age of 13 to make her teeth look better. She had to wear braces for 11 months.
  • In her early childhood, she was raised at a pig farm. However, after her father started missionary work, her family moved to Tampa, Florida.
  • In 2011, she took her first step towards her ascent to social media stardom by creating her YouTube channel.
  • In 2015, she launched her eyeshadow palette in collaboration with Morphe Cosmetics.
  • In 2021, Jaclyn was criticized by Leanne ‘The Banana Girl’ Ratcliffe because of her diet and appearance in a video. Leanne had called Jaclyn’s diet ‘incredibly unsafe’.
  • In December 2021, Jaclyn announced that she was taking a break from YouTube.
