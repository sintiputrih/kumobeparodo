Who was Storage Wars' most likable star ever?

Throughout Storage Wars’ history, the show has had several popular stars. However, one of the show’s stars always seemed to be the most popular, Barry Weiss. Labeled “The Collector” on the show, the reason why Weiss was so popular was he always seemed to be having fun. Instead of worrying about making money for a business like his co-stars, Weiss always seemed excited to find cool stuff and that became infectious for viewers.
