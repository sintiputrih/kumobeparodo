Katie Holmess Easy Off-Duty Look Includes a Floral Slipdress

Katie Holmes continues to provide endless summer outfit inspiration with her string of street-style moments.

For her latest outing in New York City yesterday, the Dawson’s Creek alum put an urban twist on the cottagecore trend. Holmes looked lovely in a black slipdress with a gathered bust and a tiered midi skirt. Covering the entire piece was a delicate peach floral pattern, which coordinated with her strappy pink platform sandals. The actor continued to accessorize with a large pale yellow shoulder bag, black sunglasses, and long chain necklaces.

TheImageDirect.com

As the season transition from spring to summer, so too has Holmes’s wardrobe. Over the past month, the actor has adapted her signature minimalistic and monochromatic style for the warming weather.

For instance, Holmes stayed cool and casual in a cropped white collared button-down, beige mesh top, and wide-leg blue jeans while attending a Pride event earlier this month. She accessorized with a tan shoulder bag by Mansur Gavriel and black ballet flats.

And during the Tribeca Film Festival, the Rare Objects director again repeated that outfit formula, this time relying on an array of Chanel pieces. At the time, she stepped out in a white T-shirt, loose-fitting light wash jeans covered in faded white polka dots, a black tweed jacket, a black quilted crossbody bag, and gold jewelry.

Holmes previously talked to Bazaar.com about her sense of style.

“We live in a world where we have a premiere and you want to look nice, and it turns into being a fashion person, and I’m like, ‘Well, I just like to look nice, I’m not really a fashion person.’ We all just want to look good,” she said.
