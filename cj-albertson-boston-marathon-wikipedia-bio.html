Who Is CJ Albertson From Boston Marathon 2022? Few Things To Know About The Track Athlete And Coach

CJ Albertson is a track and field athlete who will run in the Boston Marathon 2022. He is originally from Fresno, California.

CJ Albertson is a professional runner competing in the Boston Marathon 2022. The race will be scheduled to be held in Massachusetts on April 18.

Albertson had recently made his Boston debut in the autumn race last year. On the Men's Marathon, he is presently ranked 272nd in the world.

The 126th Boston Marathon airs live on Monday at 8:30 a.m. ET on the USA network. A total of 30,000 runners are allowed to compete.

Who Is CJ Albertson From Boston Marathon? His Wikipedia

CJ Albertson is originally from Fresno, a city in California. He attended Buchanan High School for his education.

For his higher education, the runner had attended Arizona State University, where he completed his major in Exercise and Physical Education.

During his time as a bear, he had competed in the 7-team Valley Championships. Back in the days, he was a 2-time Fresno Bee Runner of the Year

Before going to Arizona State University, he won the B'nai B'rith award to finish off his senior year.
