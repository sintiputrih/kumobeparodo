After that, She appeared in the series The Hills from the year 2007. to 2010. She has continued as a cast member of The Hills until the series finale on 13 July 2010. From the year 2011 to 2015, Lo was cast in movies and TV series like Rachel Ray (2011), Chelsea lately (2011), I'm Positive (2012), Fashion News Live (2008-2013), and Unique Sweets (2015). 

In 2016, she founded a lifestyle brand that focused on women's hygiene and self-care products, Love Wellness.

Camp Confessions (2019) and The Hills: New Beginnings (2019) are also TV series featured by Lo Bosworth.

Who Is Lo Bosworth?
