Little People Big World: Zach Pranks Bachelor Party With Naked Jeremy

All of him! Jeremy Roloff gets red in the face thanks to a prank from his twin brother, Zach, at his bachelor party in TLC’s latest Little People Big World special.

“A big part of a bachelor party is definitely always getting pranked and it was one of those things where they wanted to embarrass me, because I guess they don’t think I get embarrassed enough,” Jeremy says in Us Weekly‘s exclusive clip from Little People Big World: Jeremy and Audrey’s Journey to I Do.

The now-married 25-year-old reality star was hanging with his brother and his friends after a fun day of paintballing when Zach took off on an ATV with a mischievous look on his face.

In the clip, Zach, now engaged himself, grabs a life-sized photo of his brother he’d had made especially for the occasion — the twist? Jeremy is naked in the photo, with his bare backside on full display.

Jer and his BFF had posed for some nude photos with his friends, Zach explains. “They went to some park and they were doing some pictures naked,” he says. He then takes the photo back to the gang, who all laugh as their friend’s face flushes with embarrassment.
