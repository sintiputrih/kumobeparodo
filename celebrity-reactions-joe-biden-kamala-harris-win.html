Celebrity Reactions to Joe Biden and Kamala Harris's Election Win

People around the world are celebrating Joe Biden and Kamala Harris's historic win. While Biden is set to be the next President of the United States, Harris has made history as the first woman to ever hold the role as America's Vice President. She's also the first Black woman and first South Asian woman to be elected as America's VP, making history in the process.

Across America, Biden-Harris supporters have taken to the streets and Twitter to share their joyous reactions. A plethora of celebrities have also shared congratulatory messages online, celebrating Biden's victory, and sharing their excitement about Harris's momentous appointment.
