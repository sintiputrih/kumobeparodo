Austin Majors - Trivia, Family, Bio

Actor who began playing the character Theo Sipowicz on the television series NYPD Blue when he was three years old. He also made appearances on such TV shows as ER, According to Jim, and NCIS. His last on-screen role was in an episode of How I Met Your Mother.

Before Fame

He started his career as an actor in television commercials as a toddler.

Trivia

He became a member of Kids With A Cause in 2005. In February of 2023, after a struggle with drugs and homelessness, he passed away at 27. 

Family Life

He had a younger sister named Kali, who is also an actor.

Associated With

He acted in the show NYPD Blue with Dennis Franz.
