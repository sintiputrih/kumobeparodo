Who is Mary O Connor? Know everything about Charlie Bird’s first wife, including her current whereabouts and children.

Most people know Mary O Connor as the ex-wife of the veteran Irish journalist and broadcaster, Charlie Bird.

Charlie is the former chief news and Washington correspondent of RTÉ News and Current Affairs. He began his journalism journey with RTÉ in the mid-1970s and retired on 26 August 2012. In 2002, He received an honorary doctorate from University College Dublin.

Since his retirement, he took the job of a documentary maker. He also worked on a few writing projects.

On 27 October 2021, Charlie disclosed that he got diagnosed with Motor Neuron Disease. It is an uncommon condition with no cure that affects the brain and nerves. He also said that he hid from his friends at RTÉ for a long time.

Who Is Mary O Connor?

Mary O Connor is the first wife of the journalist Charlie Bird.

The two got married in 1974 and had two kids together. Furthermore, she was the one to inspire her ex-husband to pursue broadcast journalism. Regardless, the two left each other, which came to be after Charlie’s long-time affair with Carole Coleman (present wife since May 2016).

Mary’s children are all adults, to say, she is a grandma now. Whatsmore, she and her husband seem to have mutual responsibilities towards their children.

Even though their marriage met an unfortunate end in 1998 after 24 years together, Charlie says that the two are good friends. Reports suggest that they meet on family occasions and birthdays. A festival like Christmas is also an occasion when they share a few drinks.

Charlie’s 2006 book, This Is Charlie Bird, talked about their marriage and its end.

Besides a little about Mary’s married life, Mary never revealed much including, her parents, educational qualifications, and professional background.

Regarding her age, it is probably between 65 to 75 years, while her ex-husband is 72.
