Sprigatito (Japanese: ニャオハ Nyahoja) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Scarlet & Violet expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the Scarlet & Violet expansion, first released as both a Mirage Holofoil and Non Holofoil card in the ex Starter Set Sprigatito & Lucario ex. A Mirage Holofoil print of the English card was made available in the Fuecoco and Quaxly versions of the Paldea Collection, released January 6, 2023. It was later reprinted as a Confetti Holofoil in the English McDonald's Collection 2023.

This card is also released as a Traditional Chinese-exclusive SV-P Promotional card numbered 116/SV-P, included in the Pokémon Horizons: The Series Special Card Set (Traditional Chinese: 特別卡組 寶可夢地平線：系列 Special Card Set Pokémon Horizons: The Series). This product is a tie-in for the Pokémon Horizons: The Series anime, and is released on August 2, 2024, the same date on which the Stellar Miracle expansion releases in Taiwan and Hong Kong. The card set includes promo cards for Sprigatito and Fuecoco, a Terapagos and Friends Jumbo promo card, and 10 booster packs of Stellar Miracle.[1][2][3] All promo cards included in the card set feature the Pokémon Horizons: The Series logo stamped on the card artwork.

Trivia

Origin

Scratch and Leafage are both moves in the Pokémon games that Sprigatito can learn. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Scarlet. The Traditional Chinese 116/SV-P Promotional print features Liko, one of the main characters of Pokémon Horizons: The Series, whose first partner Pokémon is Sprigatito.
