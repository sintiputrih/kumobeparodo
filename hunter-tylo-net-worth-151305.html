Hunter Tylo Net Worth

Hunter Tylo is an American actress who was born on July 3, 1962 in Fort Worth, TX. She is best known for her role as Dr. Taylor Hayes on the daytime soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful, which she has been playing for over 20 years.
