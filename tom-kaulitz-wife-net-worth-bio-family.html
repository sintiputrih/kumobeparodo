What Is Tom Kaulitz's Net Worth?

As of now, the legendary German-guitarist, Tom Kaulitz has the jawbreaking net worth of $25 million. He came to limelight as the guitarist of the band named Tokio Hotel.

Tom and his twin brother, Bill, got enrolled in music since their young age. Later, they formed a band named Devilish along with two other members and came with their self-titled debut album in 2001. Then, the group changed their name to Tokio Hotel and also signed a record deal with the Universal Music Groups' Interscope Records in 2005.

With the Universal Music Group, the band has released Schrei (2005), studio albums (2007 and 2009), best-of album (2010), and two live albums.

Besides that, Tom has appeared in the 1994 TV movie Verruckt Nach dir and also in the Reebok shoe commercial (2010).

Tom Kaulitz's Bio And Family Details

Born as Tom Kaulitz-Trümper in 1989 in Leipzig, Germany, Tom Kaulitz celebrates his birthday on 1 September.

His parents, Simone and Jorg Kaulitz raised him along with his identical twin brother, Bill Kaulitz, in Leipzig during his childhood. The twin brothers are widely known as the frontmen of the band, Tokio Hotel.
