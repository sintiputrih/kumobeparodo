Dagen McDowell- Professional Career

McDowell worked as a dressing room attendant before pursuing a career in journalism. She worked as a salesgirl at a golf course, selling beer. She began her career as a Financial Journalist at Institutional Investor’s Newsletter Division. Later, she worked for the ‘Smart-Money’ magazine, which was published by a ‘Wall Street periodical.’ The magazine is on personal matters. She has been a business journalist for the ‘Fox News channel’ since 2003. She also appears on the chat show ‘Your World with Neil Cavuto’ regularly. The show focuses on stock market fluctuations as well as political and other variables that may influence stock values.

She began her anchoring career in 2007 and debuted as an anchor on the show ‘Markets Now,’ which Dagen hosts from 11 p.m. to 12 a.m. She then appeared as a weekly panelist on the business analysis show ‘Cashin’ In.’ She also featured as a semi-panelist on the daytime news and conversation show ‘Outnumbered,’ as well as on the American talk show ‘The Five.’ In 2016, she took over as host of the business analysis program ‘Bulls and Bears’ from Brenda Buttner.
