Betty Gabriel Biography, Husband Or Boyfriend And Family Facts

Betty Gabriel made her acting debut in 2011 on the set of In Memoriam – a TV drama where she played her first major role, but she has been part of the movie industry since 2009. The upcoming star appeared in small but recurring roles in the production Westworld as she progressed in her career. 2016 was a very good year for her as she landed a starring role in The Purge: Election Year.

Barely a year after her stint on the set of The Purge: Election Year, Betty Gabriel was celebrated for her exceptional performance in the horror movie Get Out – an Oscar award-winning film where she gave life to the character of Georgina. Furthermore, via the same movie, she became one of the contenders for the Academy Awards of 2018 in the category of Best Supporting Actress by The New York Times which also observed her role as the Best Performance of 2017. She has appeared in several other productions, among which are Unfriended: Dark Web, Diverted Eden, Upgrade (2018), Experimenter (2015), He’s Way More Famous Than You (2013), Good Girls Revolt (2015 – 2016) and Beyond Skyline (2017). Fans are eager to watch her bring the character of the FBI agent Naya Temple to life in Counterpart television series.

Betty Gabriel’s Biography

Recently, the movie actress’ bio has been a trending topic with fans and the general public expressing their desire to know more. But, Betty is yet to break her silence on the issue, even though she keeps getting tickles from the buzzes of curiosity to voice out.

However, it is public knowledge that her place of birth is Washington D.C but she spent her formative years in Hyattsville, MD, and Pittsburgh. The popular actress’ ethnicity is Afro-American and it came to light via a tweet that her birthday falls on the 14th of October but her exact year of birth continues to elude the media.

Her educational background is an admirable one because, after her high school graduation, she joined IOWA University to study Animal Science/Vet, graduating with a Bachelors degree. In a bid to study acting, she moved to Chicago a while later and put many years into dancing and acting at various popular theaters like Steppenwolf and the Victory Gardens. Subsequently, she made the decision to proceed to the next level of her career, emerging as a Julliard graduate in no time at all. Although she is still trying to climb the success ladder, Betty Gabriel already has a short wiki page exclusive to her name.
