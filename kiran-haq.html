Career

Before pursuing a career in acting, Kiran Haq worked as a model and also appeared in many television commercials. Kiran made her television debut as a supporting role in the Pakistani television drama ‘Tere Pehlu Mein’ as Kiran, aired on Geo Entertainment in  2010. In the following year, she appeared on Hum TV’s comedy television drama ‘Akbari Asghari’ as Shabbo. Thereafter, she became part of many popular shows like Akbari Asghari, Ahmed Habib Ki Betiyan, and Bano Bazar. Kiran gained popularity after her performance in the television serial ‘Main Marr Gaye Shaukat Ali.’

Kiran Haq established herself as a lead actor in the entertainment industry after delivering a remarkable performance in A Plus Entertainment’s ‘Shehr-e-Ajnabi;’ The show was aired on A-Plus Entertainment in the year 2014 and Kiran portrayed the character of Ufaq in the drama. Next, she appeared in television shows ‘Khaali Haath’ as Sobia and ‘Dar Si Jaati Hai Sila’ as Nadia which made her a household name in no time. Thereafter, she worked on several commercially successful projects including Tum Mere Kya Ho, Sangdil, Khaali Haath, Dil Tanha Tanha, and Naulakha.

Kiran Haq was going to make her film debut with Faisal Qureshi’s entertainment film, ‘Money Back Guarantee,’ which was all set to be released on 22 May 2020 but was rescheduled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The film also features Fawad Khan, Hina Dilpazir, Ali Safina, Wasim Akram, Shayan Khan, Mikaal Zulfiqar, Gohar Rasheed, and Jan Rambo.
