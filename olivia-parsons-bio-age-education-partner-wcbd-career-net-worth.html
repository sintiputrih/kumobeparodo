Olivia Parsons Biography

Olivia Parsons is an American journalist working as a weekend morning news anchor for WCBD- TV News 2 which is a dual NBC/CW+-affiliated television station located in Charleston, SC in the United States of America. Before joining WCBD News 2, Parsons worked as an associate producer at WFTV Channel 9 ABC.

Furthermore, she is responsible for generating content for weekend morning shows and interviewing guests during the live segments.

During her free time, Parsons enjoys hanging out at one of the many restaurants on Shem Creek, shopping down King Street, as well as watching the latest chick-flick at the Mount Pleasant movie theater.

Olivia Parsons Career

Olivia started her on-air career officially at Fox 35 WOFL in Orlando, FL as a reporter intern. She served from June 2017 to Aug 2017 and some of her duties were to write stories for the evening newscast and shooting videos for several reporters.

Later in 2018, Olivia moved to Wftv Channel 9 in Orlando, FL where she served as an associate producer. There, she did coverage of stories that ranged from breaking news, hostage situations to local elections, and natural disasters. Additionally, the reporter would produce scripts for the daybreak show as well as cueing live shots for reporters in the field.

Currently, Parsons works at WCBD- TV in Charleston, SC as a weekend morning news anchor and a reporter during the week. Olivia is responsible for generating content for weekend morning shows and interviewing guests during the live segments.
