Biography

Rodolfo Mancuso (born February 28, 1992), addressed professionally as Rudy Mancuso, is a trailblazer in the American entertainment industry. He is an American actor, Youtuber, social media personality, and musician. He is best known for making comedy skits with his friends on YouTube and Vine.

He started making videos with friends in high school. They would film each other doing stunts and making jokes. 

In 2012, Rudy Mancuso started his channel called Awkward Puppets. The channel features skits with puppets and has amassed over 5 million subscribers.

Early Life & Education

Rudy Mancuso was born to Italian-American parents in Glen Ridge, New Jersey. He was born on the 28th of February, 1992, in Glen Ridge, New Jersey, United States.

His Italian father and Brazilian mother raised him with his sister Marianna Mancuso. 

Rudy Mancuso went to Glen Ridge High School and Rutgers University, Newark for his education. He spent time in Rio de Janeiro and is conversant in Portuguese. He began playing the piano when he was five years old.

Rudy Mancuso‘s girlfriend is a model, singer and actress named Maia Mitchell; the duo has been dating since 2015. Rumor has it that they split, but none of them have come out to debunk the gossip.

Rudy Mancuso has no married wife or kids.

Career

Rudy Mancuso started his YouTube channel in 2016 after partnering with Shots Studios to create videos. Drunk History on Comedy Central and Outpost on HBO have both featured him. Along with Mariah Carey and DJ Khaled, he also appeared on YouTube Red’s Keys of Christmas.

Rudy Mancuso and Shots Studios own Awkward Puppets, a puppet series available on YouTube. In 2017, he served as the opening act for Justin Bieber‘s Purpose World Tour in Brazil.

On June 17, 2017, Rudy Mancuso was invited to walk in Dolce & Gabbana’s Spring/Summer 2018 Milan Men’s Fashion Week show. He performed and accepted an award at the 2017 MTV Millennial Awards in Mexico.

Rudy Mancuso performed two days in a row at Villa Mix, Brazil’s largest music event, in Goiânia in July 2017. He collaborated with songwriter/producer Poo Bear on his first single, Black & White, which was released on October 6, 2017. 

Rudy Mancuso played at the Villa Mix festival in So Paulo on October 7, 2017.
