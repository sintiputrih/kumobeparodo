Micael dos Santos Silva Height And Weight

Micael dos Santos Silva (born August 12, 2000) is a Brazilian professional footballer who now plays as a centre-back for Major League Soccer club Houston Dynamo.

Micael began his playing career after being born in Presidente Prudente, So Paulo, at Limeira-based clubs Internacional and Independent. He joined Atlético Mineiro’s youth academy in 2019, and the following year he won the Campeonato Brasileiro Sub-20 title.

Micael made his professional debut in the Campeonato Brasileiro Série A on December 9, 2021, in a 4-3 away defeat to Grêmio.

On April 27, 2022, Micael agreed to a season-long loan with Houston Dynamo 2, the MLS club’s reserve team.

He made his MLS debut for the first squad on August 13, losing 3-2 to CF Montréal.

Houston Dynamo FC is a professional soccer team headquartered in Houston, Texas. The Houston Dynamo play in the Western Conference of Major League Soccer (MLS).

Micael dos Santos Silva is 6 feet 3 inches tall and weighs 181 pounds.
