Tim Allen children: Elizabeth Allen Dick, Katherine Allen

Timothy Alan Dick is an American actor and comedian. He is best recognized for his roles as Tim The Toolman Taylor on ABC’s Home Improvement (1991–1999) and Mike Baxter on ABC/Last Fox’s Man Standing (2011–2021).

In the Toy Story franchise, he voices Buzz Lightyear, and in the Santa Clause franchise (1994–2006), he plays Scott Calvin and Santa Claus. Tropical Snow (1988), Galaxy Quest (1999), Joe Somebody (2001), Zoom (2006), Wild Hogs (2007), The Six Wives of Henry Lefay (2009), Crazy on the Outside (2010), 3 Geezers! (2013), and El Camino Christmas (2014) are among Allen’s other films (2017).

Tim Allen children: Elizabeth Allen Dick, Katherine Allen

Elizabeth Allen Dick and Katherine Allen are his children.

On April 7, 1984, he married Laura Deibel. Katherine was born in December 1989.

He and Deibel divorced in 2003 after formally splitting up in 1999.

On October 7, 2006, Allen married actress Jane Hajduk in a tiny private wedding in Grand Lake, Colorado. They’d been together for five years. Elizabeth was born in March 2009.
