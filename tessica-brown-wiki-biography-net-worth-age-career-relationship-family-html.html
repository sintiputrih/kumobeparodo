Tessica Brown: Wiki and Biography

  • Tessica Brown, also referred to as the Gorilla Glue Girl, was born on October 20, 1981, in Violet, Louisiana, United States.

The Gorilla Glue Incident and its Aftermath

  • The Gorilla Glue incident unfolded when Tessica Brown uploaded a video on her TikTok account, demonstrating her use of Gorilla Glue as a substitute for hair spray. Little did she know that this decision would have significant consequences.
  • Tessica’s video quickly gained traction on social media platforms, resulting in her newfound fame as the Gorilla Glue Girl. Her desperate attempts to remove the glue from her hair, including washing it multiple times and seeking medical assistance, garnered significant attention.
  • Despite the efforts of doctors, who even used acetone on her scalp, the glue remained stubbornly attached. The ordeal caused Tessica immense pain and distress, ultimately leading to a video in which her hairdresser used kitchen scissors to cut off her ponytail.
  • Tessica created a GoFundMe page to raise funds for her treatment. Eventually, she traveled to Los Angeles to consult with Dr. Michael Obeng, a Beverly Hills plastic surgeon. After a four-hour procedure involving a mixture of medical-grade adhesive, aloe vera, olive oil, and acetone, Tessica was finally free from the glue’s grip.
