Lana’s marriage and dating

Lana does have a history when it comes to her love life, and it is quite random as well.

They had a very on and off kind of relationship, but they finally broke up in November 2015 because Lana wanted to settle down with him, but Francesco was not ready yet.

Lana then briefly started dating G-Eazy, who is a rapper and singer. They were seen at Coachella in 2017 kissing each other and were very close. They had a very short relationship which lasted only five months.

After the split with G-Eazy, Lana started to date Sean “Sticks” Larkin, who works as a police officer. They started dating in September 2019. 

Their relationship lasted for six months before breaking up in 2020. According to Lana, they are still on good terms.

She later confirmed the news regarding her relationship with Clayton in The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon. 

Lana said that she met her fiance Clayton through a dating app and had clicked right away.
