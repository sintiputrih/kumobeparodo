In 1971, Lea Loveless Maurer (née Lea E. Loveless) was born in New York City and went on to become an American competitive swimmer, Olympic champion, and former collegiate swimming coach. The United States delegation to the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain, was led by her gold medal performance in the women’s 4×100-meter medley relay, which she swam in the backstroke. In addition, she took home a bronze medal in the 100-meter backstroke event. She formerly served as the women’s swimming and diving coach for the Stanford University women’s swimming and diving team from 2005 until 2012.
