Riley Curry Hollywood Life

Bio:
Riley Curry (born Riley Elizabeth Curry on July 19, 2012 in Charlotte, North Carolina) is the adorable daughter of NBA star Stephen Curry. At just 2 years old, Riley has repeatedly stole the spotlight from her father by being her super cute self. During one of Stephen’s post-game press conferences in May 2015, Riley popped on stage because she wanted to sit on his lap. As he answered questions from journalists, Riley giggled and told him to be “be quiet.” Days later she jumped on stage during a second conference so that she could sing Big Sean‘s “Blessings” to the waiting journalists. Since then, she has continued to steal basketball fans hearts all over the world.

Best Known For:
Being Steph Curry’s daughter and enjoying the spotlight.

Personal Life:  
Since Riley is only 2 years old, check back in about 10 years (2015) to find out who she’s been crushing on.
