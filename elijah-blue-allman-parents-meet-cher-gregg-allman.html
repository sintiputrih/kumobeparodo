Elijah Blue Allman Parents: Meet Cher &amp; Gregg Allman

Elijah Blue Allman, born on July 10, 1976, into a world of music and fame, is the son of two iconic figures in the entertainment industry, Cher and Gregg Allman.

Meet Elijah Blue Allman Parents

  • Cher (Cherilyn Sarkisian):
    • Elijah’s mother, Cher, is an American singer, actress, and television personality who has achieved legendary status in the world of entertainment. With a career spanning several decades, Cher is known for her powerful voice, distinctive contralto singing range, and versatility in various facets of the entertainment industry.
  • Gregg Allman (Gregory LeNoir Allman):
    • Elijah’s father, Gregg Allman, was a celebrated American musician, singer, and songwriter. He was best known as the founder of the Allman Brothers Band, a pioneering Southern rock group that played a significant role in shaping the genre. Gregg Allman’s contributions to the world of music earned him a place among the rock legends.
