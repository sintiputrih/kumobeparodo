Snowman with dubiously-placed carrot sparks debate in mums group -Kidspot

A mum has posted a photo of her new inflatable snowman with a ‘happy’ carrot, worrying about her neighbour’s reaction. But people were thinking of the children.

We all know Christmas is mostly for kids, but sometimes adults need a little something for themselves.

So we have mistletoe and catching mummy kissing Santa Claus.. but one woman may have taken things a bit further with her new inflatable and NSFW or kids, snowman.

The offending snowman

Posting in a Christmas mums Facebook group, a woman asked members if they would be offended by her new inflatable snowman… which sports a cheeky expression and a carrot… placed as an erect penis instead of a nose.

“Would you be offended if you saw this on your street?”, the woman asked the group, worried about her neighbour’s reaction.

But most responses unexpectedly were more concerned about children.

“What? It’s cold.” Source: Facebook

Think of the children

Most of the mums in the group said they wouldn’t want their kids to see this, um, erected in someone’s front yard.

“My kids would ask too many questions I’m not yet emotionally prepared to answer,” one mum wrote.

Added another, “Christmas is for kids and shouldn’t be sexualised.”

Some agreed the snowman might be ok in the backyard, if you had teenagers who may understand the joke.

But some members berated the mum for being ‘immature’, when Christmas should be innocent.

“This is something teenagers would laugh at, not really funny,” she was told.

Another added: “Not appropriate for the front yard at all.”

“Ok fine, is this G-rated (and boring) carrot placement better?” Source: iStock

Many LOL’d

Despite the concerns about the X-rated snowman, members admitted it gave them a giggle, and LOL abounded in the comments.

“My husband and I would LOL at this,” one mum admitted.

“I would LOL if my neighbour got offended at this,” another added.
