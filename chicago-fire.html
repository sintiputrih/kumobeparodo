'Chicago Fire' Latest News and Updates

The first installment of Dick Wolf's Chicago franchise, Chicago Fire follows the men and women at Firehouse 51 in the Windy City.

The series inspired the successful spin-offs Chicago Med and Chicago P.D. and has also had crossover episodes with those dramas and Law and Order: SVU.
