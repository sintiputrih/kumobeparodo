Ariana Grande's New Boyfriend Makes Cameo in &quot;Rain on Me&quot; Sketch

  • Ariana Grande's boyfriend, burgeoning real estate tycoon Dalton Gomez, makes a quick appearance in a new video sketch the singer recorded with Lady Gaga.
  • Grande and Gomez have been seeing each other since January and have been quarantining together amid the ongoing coronavirus pandemic.

Dalton Gomez is continuing to make blink-and-you'll-miss-him appearances in his girlfriend Ariana Grande's latest videos.

Yesterday, both Grande and Lady Gaga shared a hilarious Weather Channel–themed sketch to their social media accounts to promote their new single, "Rain on Me," and its music video. In the quick clip, Gaga and Grande take turns reporting the weather and showing their excitement over an uncharacteristic downpour in Southern California. When Gaga throws it over to Grande—who's "reporting live" from her Beverly Hills home—her newest beau, budding real estate tycoon Dalton Gomez, can be seen helping her perfect her camera shot.

This video isn't the first time Grande's boyfriend has made an on-screen cameo alongside the pop star. Earlier this month, Grande confirmed speculation of their romance when Gomez appeared toward the end of the music video for "Stuck with U," with the pair dancing together and embracing while staying home amid the ongoing coronavirus pandemic.

The brief but memorable joint public appearances are rare, considering Grande is seemingly trying keep things low-key following the dissolution of her highly publicized engagement to Saturday Night Live star Pete Davidson. "Ariana doesn't want to do another public relationship so she is trying to keep this one quiet," a source previously told People, "but she seems very happy with Dalton."
