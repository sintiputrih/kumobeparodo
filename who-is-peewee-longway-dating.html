Quincy Lamont Williams, known by his stage name Peewee Longway is an American hip hop recording artist from Atlanta, Georgia who was one of the last signees of 1017 Records before founder Gucci Mane’s 20132016 imprisonment. He is also the nephew of Brick Squad member Cold Blooded Da Don who introduced him to Gucci Mane. The artist’s most celebrated releases to date are his mixtape The Blue M&M and his collaboration with Young Thug, “Loaded”. The Blue M&M also featured the singles “Sneakin n Geekin” and “Servin Lean” featuring A$AP Rocky. He is also a member of a rap group with Gucci Mane and Young Dolph, collectively known as “Felix Brothers”. They released their debut project in July 2014.

His debut mixtape Money, Pounds, Ammunition was released in March of 2013. He put out two more mixtapes that same year called Running Around The Lobby and Lobby Runners.
