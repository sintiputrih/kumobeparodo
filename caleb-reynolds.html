Caleb Reynolds Big Brother, Bio, Wiki, Age, Height, Wife, and Net Worth

Caleb Reynolds Bio | Wiki

Caleb Reynolds ( Full name: Caleb Matthew Reynolds) is an American reality show star from Hopkinsville, Kentucky. He is best known for his participation in the sixteenth season of the reality series called Big Brother. In the competition, Caleb placed fourth. Also, he competed in the 32nd season of Survivor; Survivor: Kaôh Rōng.  However, Caleb was medically evacuated on day 9 of the competition after collapsing from heatstroke as a result of extreme heat. His condition ended his game and finished in 15th place.

Caleb won his fourth HOH of the season in week 12. Only counting official or permanent HOHs, he is tied with the winner of season 5 Drew Daniels, third place on season 7 Janelle Pierzina, winner of season 12 Hayden Moss, winner of season 13 Rachel Reilly, winner of season 14 Ian Terry, eighth place on season 15 Aaryn Gries, third place on season 17 Vanessa Rousso, and also the winner of season 17 Steve Moses for the most HOHs in a single season.

Then, Caleb nominated Frankie and Victoria for eviction. HOH Derrick nominated Caleb and Victoria for eviction in week 12. Although Cody won the veto, he decided not to use it and left the nominations the same. Caleb was evicted on day 90 by Cody, placing him in 4th Place. Due to his obsession with Amber, Caleb was not liked by some houseguests at the beginning of the game, especially Cody and Zach.

He had a well-known unrequited crush on Amber which other housemates regard as “creepy and obsessive”. However, he managed to obtain a friendship with the two. Also, Caleb had close friendships with Amber, Frankie, Donny, Devin, Brittany, and Derrick.  The reality tv star finally voted for Derrick to win Big Brother.

Caleb Reynolds Big Brother

He participated in the sixteenth season of Big Brother. After applying for The Amazing Race with his friend, Caleb was recruited. He took part in the second group which entered the house and became the Head of Household after he won the “Over the Coals” competition.  Later, Caleb became the first official HoH after his nominees Donny and Paola failed to win the “Battle of the Block” competition. He came up with a “Bomb Squad” alliance with his fellow HoH Frankie. the alliance includes  Christine, Amber, and all of the men apart from Donny and Hayden.

Caleb volunteered for Derrick to nominate him in week 3 so Derrick and Nicole could backdoor Devin; then, Derrick nominated Caleb and Jocasta. He was nominated alongside Victoria in week 6 by Donny, who was HOH along with Nicole but Caleb and Victoria won Battle Of The Block, dethroning Donny and also securing their safety from the block.

Caleb had to endure three punishments whatsoever. Those punishments included eating slop for 2 weeks, and Adam and Eve were chained to Victoria in leafy costumes for 48 good hours. Lastly, one had to shave their head. He won his second HOH of the season on day 49 making him the first person to officially hold the title twice.

Caleb nominated Hayden and Donny for eviction but Donny emerged as the POV winner and Caleb named Hayden’s showmantic partner called Nicole as the replacement nominee causing Hayden’s eviction. Caleb won his third HOH of the season on day 70. It was such an endurance competition that made him the first person to be HOH officially three times. Frankie and Nicole had four and three HOHs respectively and were dethroned twice. Caleb nominated Nicole and Christine for eviction. However, Christine won POV so Caleb named Victoria the replacement nominee alongside Nicole hoping that Nicole would be evicted a second time.

Caleb Reynolds Survivor Seasons | Survivor Heat Stroke

He took part as a contestant on Survivor: Kaôh Rōng in 2015. Caleb was placed on the season’s Beauty (Gondol) tribe.
