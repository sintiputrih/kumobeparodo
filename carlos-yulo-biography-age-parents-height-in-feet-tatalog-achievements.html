Carlos Yulo is a famous Filipino artistic gymnast who has won bronze and gold at the World Artistic Gymnastics Championships.

Carlos Yulo was born on February 16, 2000, in Malate, Manila, Philippines. His parents are Mark Andrew Yulo and Angelica Yulo. He is the second of five children. Carlos’ brother Drew, is also a gymnast.

Carlos Yulo started his education at the Aurora A. Quezon Elementary School for his primary education in Manila. At the school, he was already training for the Philippine National Games as part of the National Capital Region’s gymnastics team.

Carlos Yulo then proceeded to the Adamson University in Ermita for his secondary education with the support of the  Gymnastics Association of the Philippines.
