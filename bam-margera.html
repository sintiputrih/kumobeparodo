Bam Margera aka Brandon “Bam” Margera is an American stunt performer, television personality, actor, writer, director and skateboarder. As of 2022, Bam Margera’s net worth is $5 million. He rose to fame with MTV’s comedy reality show Jackass during the early 2000s. He also became a part of the seasons and spin-offs of this series.

Bam Margera Wiki/Biography

Born on 28 September 1979, Bam Margera’s age is 43 Years Old as of 2022. He was born and raised in West Chester, Pennsylvania, United States. He holds an American nationality and he has belief in Christian religion.

Bam Margera Childhood Pic

He joined a school for proper education, but after the completion of junior year, he left the school. His mother took the responsibility to educate him through homeschooling. He later appeared in the four exams by General Educational Development.

After passing these exams, he got the GED certificate, a legal qualification the same as a high school degree in the United States. His original name is Brandon Margera. But his grandfather gave him the nickname “ Bam Bam” because, as a child, he used to run into walls and bam himself.
