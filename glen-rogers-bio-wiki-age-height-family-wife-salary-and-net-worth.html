Glen Rogers Serial Killer

Glen is the main suspect in the stabbing or strangling of an elderly man from Ohio in 1993 and four women in California, Mississippi, Florida, and Louisiana. He once claimed the number of murders was closer to 70, but then recanted his statement, claiming he was joking and had not committed any murders.

Glen has been linked to the following murders:

  • Mark Peters (Hamilton, Ohio) – suspected victim
  • Sandra Gallagher (Los Angeles, California)
  • Linda Price (Jackson, Mississippi)
  • Tina Marie Cribbs (Tampa, Florida)
  • Andy Jiles Sutton (Bossier City, Louisiana)

Glen Rogers Documentary

Glen’s crimes and killings have been narrated in the 2012 documentary My Brother the Serial Killer. The documentary claims that Rogers killed Nicole Brown Simpson and Ron Goldman in 1994. y Brother the Serial Killer was narrated by Rogers’ brother Clay Rogers and aired on Investigation Discovery in November 2012. According to Clay, Rogers claimed that, before the murders, he had met Brown and was “going to take her down.”

Glen Rogers O. J. Simpson (Nicole Simpson)

During a correspondence that began in 2009 between Rogers and criminal profiler Anthony Meolis, Glen wrote and created paintings about his involvement with the murders.
