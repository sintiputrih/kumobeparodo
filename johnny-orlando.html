Johnny Orlando - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Single, Nationality, Facts

Table of Biography

Johnny Orlando is a Canadian Singer, Songwriter, Actor, and Social media influencer famous for using a variety of instruments like Guitar, piano, and Tuba. Johnny Orlando came into the limelight after covering Justin Biber‘s song Mistletoe on his YouTube channel.

Early Life and Childhood

Johnny Orlando a Canadian Singer, Songwriter, Actor, and Social media influencer (21 years) was born on January 24, 2003, in Ontario, Canada. His real name is Johnny Vincent Orlando. His father’s name is Dale Orlando and his mother’s name is Meredith Orlando.

He has three siblings and sisters. His older sisters are Darian and Madison and her younger sister is Lauren Orlando. His birth sign is Aquarius and belongs to the white ethnicity. There is no information regarding his education. But he seems to be graduating high school soon at his age.

Professional Life

At the early age of 8, he started his career by releasing his original song named Summertime in 2012. Then later in 2013, he released the songs Replay and Never Give Up. Then his songs Found My Girl in 2014 See The World, Right By Your Side, and You And I in 2015. However, in 2016 Let Go, Day and Night came out.

Then his songs Missing You, Everything, and The Most in 2017. What If and Last Summer in 2018 then Sleep, Piece of My Heart, Deep Down, Why, Waste My Time, and All These Parties in 2019. He released his first EP VXIIXI in 2015.

Toronto-born singer-songwriter and pop artist Orlando released his debut EP, Teenage Fever in March 2019. Having just turned 17 years old, his music has already garnered over 355 million streams to date and has earned acclaim from the likes of Billboard, Variety, Paper, Teen Vogue, Just Jared Jr., Tiger Beat, and more.

In his film career, he appeared in the film Pinocchio in the role of Pinocchio voice in 2018. Then the same year he made an appearance in the TV series Total Eclipse role of Sam. Moreover, the features in the episode of Chicken Girls and Baby Doll Records.

In 2019 he also headlined his first-ever North American and European tour in support of his EP. With a collective social following of over 22 million fans, he is at the forefront of the next generation of Canadian artists influencing the global music stage.

He signed with Universal Music Canada on May 18, 2018. Likewise, he released “Phobias” on February 21, 2020, and “SeeYou” on April 15, 2020. He has cited Justin Bieber and Shawn Mendes among his musical inspirations. He uploads his songs and covers videos on his self-titled YouTube channel where he has amassed around 4.24M subscribers.

Awards and Nominations

The multitalented star won and got a nomination for several awards throughout his short career. In 2019 he won the Juno Awards for Breakthrough Artist of the Year and MTV Europe Music Awards for Best Canadian Act. He was nominated for the Teen Choice Awards for Choice Music Web Star in 2016.

Then in 2017 and 2018 Kids’ Choice Awards for Favourite Musical Artist and Favourite Musical YT Creator. Then in 2019, he was nominated for Choice Music Web Star and was nominated for the Kids Choice Awards as Favorite Social Music Star.
