He would later go on to earn recurring roles in Snowy River: The McGregor Saga and The CW’s Reign. While playing Gideon Blackburn in Reign, he starred opposite Adelaide Kane and Megan Follows. While studying at the National Institute of Dramatic Art, he would star in productions of Romeo and Juliet and Much Ado About Nothing.
