Age, Biography and Wiki

Rogelio de la Rosa was a Filipino actor, writer, and director. He was one of the most popular actors in the Philippines during the 1940s and 1950s. He was known for his roles in films such as "Tatlong Maria" (1941), "Anak ng Espada" (1947), and "Tatlong Maria sa Amerika" (1951). He also wrote and directed several films, including "Kapag Langit ang Humatol" (1954). De la Rosa was born in Lubao, Pampanga, Philippines on 12 November 1916. He began his career as a stage actor in the 1930s, and made his film debut in 1940. He went on to appear in over 100 films, and was one of the most popular actors in the Philippines during the 1940s and 1950s. De la Rosa was married to actress Amalia Fuentes from 1956 until his death in 1986. He was also the father of actor Romeo de la Rosa. He died on 8 August 1986 in Quezon City, Philippines.
