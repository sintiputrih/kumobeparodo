Ashley Graham Is Pregnant with Twins!

Ashley Graham just revealed she’s pregnant with twins and she revealed the sex of the babies on the way, too!

The 33-year-old model announced her pregnancy with husband Justin Ervin back in July. Today, she released a video of the moment she found out she’s expecting two!!!
