Where did Mac Jones go to college? Did Mac Jones play college football?

Mac Jones is a famous American footballer born to his parents in Jacksonville, Florida, the United States as Michael McCorkle Jones. He is a quarterback for the New England Patriots of the National Football League having been drafted by the team in the 2021 NFL Draft. He was said to be one of the top quarterback prospects of the 2021 NFL Draft.

Mac is loved by many following how talented he is as a footballer. Just like you, many have been wanting to know almost everything about him, especially his educational background to know where he attended college and whether or not he played college football. Continue reading to find out as we dive into his educational background.

Where did Mac Jones go to college?

Jones after high school attended two colleges before finally graduating. After high school, he enrolled at the University of Kentucky, where he originally committed and later accepted a scholarship offer from the University of Alabama to play for the Crimson Tide.

Did Mac Jones play college football?

Jones played college football at Kentucky and Alabama.
