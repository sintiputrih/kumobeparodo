Johnny Dang Net Worth: How the King of Bling Built His Jewelry Empire

Johnny Dang is a Vietnamese-American jeweler who has earned the nickname of the King of Bling for his dazzling custom creations. He is the founder and owner of Johnny Dang and Co., a wholesale jewelry company that caters to celebrities, athletes, and hip-hop stars. He is also a rapper and a reality TV star who has appeared in several music videos and shows. But how did he achieve such success and fame? What is his net worth and how does he spend it? In this article, we will explore the life and career of Johnny Dang, as well as his sources of income and expenses.

Early Life and Education

Johnny Dang was born as Đặng Anh Tuấn in Đắk Lắk, Vietnam, in 1974. He grew up in a poor village where his father and grandfather were jewelers. He learned the craft from them and started making jewelry at a young age. In 1987, his father escaped Vietnam by boat and settled in Houston, Texas, where he opened a small jewelry shop. In 1996, Johnny and his mother and siblings joined his father in Houston. He was 23 years old at the time.

Johnny enrolled in Houston Community College to learn English and improve his skills. He also worked with his brother at a flea market, repairing watches and making custom jewelry. He soon realized that there was a huge demand for unique and flashy jewelry in the American market, especially among the hip-hop community. He decided to pursue his passion and open his own store.

Career as a Jeweler

In 1998, Johnny opened his first store, TV Jewelry, in the Sharpstown Mall in Houston. He named it after his nickname, TV Johnny, which he got from his customers who saw him on TV shows. He specialized in making grills, which are removable metal covers for teeth that are often studded with diamonds or other precious stones. He made his first grill for rapper Paul Wall, who became his friend and business partner. Together, they launched Grills by Paul Wall, a company that sold custom-made grills online.

Johnny’s reputation as a jeweler to the stars grew rapidly as he started making jewelry for other famous rappers, such as Nelly, Lil Wayne, Rick Ross, Kanye West, and Drake. He also expanded his clientele to include singers, actors, athletes, and socialites. Some of his notable customers are Beyoncé, Jay-Z, Justin Bieber, Post Malone, Travis Scott, Floyd Mayweather Jr., LeBron James, Kim Kardashian, and Kylie Jenner.

Johnny’s jewelry is known for its quality, creativity, and extravagance. He uses high-grade materials such as gold, platinum, diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds, and pearls. He can make any design imaginable, from simple chains and rings to elaborate pendants and bracelets. He can also customize any item with personal details such as names, initials, logos, or portraits. For example, he teamed up with Nike to make a pair of Air Force 1 sneakers that have 11 carats of diamonds on them. He also worked with Supreme to make a gold pendant that features their logo in diamonds.

In 2016, Johnny moved his business from the Sharpstown Mall to a standalone location on Richmond Avenue in Houston. The new store is bigger and more luxurious than the previous one. Career as a Rapper

Besides being a jeweler, Johnny is also a rapper who goes by the name of TV Johnny or King Johnny. He started rapping as a hobby but soon realized that he had talent and potential. He released his first mixtape in 2005 called The Takeover Vol. 1: The King of Grills. He followed it up with several other mixtapes and albums, such as The Takeover Vol. 2: The King of Bling, The Takeover Vol. 3: The King of Ice, and The Takeover Vol. 4: The King of Diamonds.
