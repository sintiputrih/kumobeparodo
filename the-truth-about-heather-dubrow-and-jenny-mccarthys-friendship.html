The Truth About Heather Dubrow And Jenny McCarthy's Friendship

As it turns out, Heather Dubrow and Jenny McCarthy are still big-time pals! And McCarthy is also close with Dubrow's husband and highly acclaimed plastic surgeon, Dr. Terry Dubrow. He even stepped in to "save" McCarthy from the horrors of botched surgery (via The Daily Dish).

McCarthy took to Twitter in November of 2015 to thank her BFF's husband for coming in clutch during her time of need. "Thank you @DrDubrow You save me from being #Botched. You are the BEST," McCarthy penned in the tweet. After all, friends don't let friends get bad botched plastic surgery, right? 

There's still no word as to whether McCarthy employs The Dubrow Diet plan that is purported to help stop the aging process into her own lifestyle, but it appears that the ladies are both doing something to reverse the aging process. Cheers to their friendship getting older while they simultaneously turn back the hands of time.
