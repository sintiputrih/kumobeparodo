Justin Theroux praises ex-wife Jennifer Anistons LolaVie haircare line

Justin Theroux is a supportive ex.

The “Leftovers” actor, 50, took to his Instagram Stories to praise ex-wife Jennifer Aniston’s new haircare line, LolaVie.

“LolaVie WORKS. Thank you b & @lolavie,” he captioned a photo of the brand’s Perfecting Leave-In ($29) — the second product from Aniston’s line, which she launched in September.

After teasing the brand, the 52-year-old “Friends” favorite released a Glossing Detangler ($25), calling it “the Swiss army knife of hair products.”

Aniston and Theroux wed in 2015 and split in 2018, but they often show love for each other on birthdays and other special occasions.

Earlier this year, Theroux said the two are still extremely close. “We don’t talk every day, but we call each other. We FaceTime. We text,” he told Esquire. “I’m sincere when I say that I cherish our friendship.”

While Aniston has been single since her divorce, she recently revealed that she’s ready to date again — and is looking outside of Hollywood circles.

“No one of importance has hit my radar yet but I think it’s time. I think I’m ready to share myself with another,” the “Morning Show” actress said in September.

“The ease at which the conversation flows the first time, that’s kind of a good indicator,” she said of her qualifications for a man, adding, “Confidence, but not a cocky-ness. Humor, please, I beg of you. Generous, kind to people. You know…very few necessities required.”

One clear turn-off for Aniston? Bad hair.
