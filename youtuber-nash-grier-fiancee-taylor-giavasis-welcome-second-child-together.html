YouTuber Nash Grier &amp; Fiancee Taylor Giavasis Welcome Second Child Together

Nash Grier is now a dad of two!

On Saturday (July 30), the 24-year-old YouTuber and his fiancee Taylor Giavasis took to social media to announce that their second daughter was born on Wednesday, July 27.

Click inside to find out her name…

Nash took to his Instagram to reveal that he and Taylor named their daughter Noa.

Over on her own Instagram, Taylor shared tons of photos from her home birth.

“On July 27th after less than 3 hours of labor my baby girl entered the world. I am sure I will have more to say at a later time but for now all I can say is birth is wild and yet all things magical,” Taylor wrote.

“Thank you to my midwife who I can’t live without, to my doula who gives the best counter pressure,” Taylor continued. “My mother who played with my very alert toddler at 3 am. Most importantly, thank you to my life partner for making this child with me, for physically holding me up while I brought her into the world, and for promptly throwing out the rug I s–t and bled all over.”

Along with Noa, Nash and Taylor are parents to daughter Malakai, who was born in September 2019.

Nash and Taylor started dating in 2015 and got engaged four years later.

Congrats to the happy family!
