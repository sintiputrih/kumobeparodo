The Meaning Behind The Song: Dreams to Dream (Finale Version) by Linda Ronstadt

TitleDreams to Dream (Finale Version)
ArtistLinda Ronstadt
Writer/ComposerJames Horner & Will Jennings
AlbumAn American Tail: Fievel Goes West (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack) (1991)
Release Date1991

When I hear the beautiful ballad “Dreams to Dream (Finale Version)” by Linda Ronstadt, I am immediately transported to a world of dreams and imagination. The lyrics and melody captivate me, taking me on a journey through the power of dreams. This song holds a special place in my heart, as it reminds me of the importance of holding onto our dreams and the endless possibilities they hold.

The verses of the song speak to the feeling of being lost and unheard in the world. Linda Ronstadt’s emotive delivery conveys a sense of vulnerability and longing. The lines “I lose my way, No one cares, The words I say, No one hears” resonate with anyone who has ever felt invisible or dismissed.

However, the chorus brings a glimmer of hope and resilience. It proclaims that even when the world goes wrong, dreams have the power to make it right. “Dreams to dream in the dark of the night, When the world goes wrong, I can still make it right” reminds us that our dreams can be a guiding light in the darkest of times.

The song also explores the idea that dreams are deeply personal and can bring us to a world that no one else sees. The lines “Come with me, You will see what I mean, There’s a world inside, No one else ever sees” provoke a sense of curiosity and wonder. It encourages us to embrace our imagination and explore the possibilities that lie within our dreams.

In my own life, “Dreams to Dream” has served as a source of inspiration and motivation. It reminds me to never let go of my dreams and to hold onto them with all my might. The lines “Don’t let go, If you stay close to me, In my dreams tonight, You will see what I see” inspire me to keep working towards my goals, knowing that they have the potential to become a reality.

Personally, I associate this song with a particular period of my life when I was facing numerous challenges and obstacles. It was a time when I felt lost and uncertain about my future. However, whenever I listened to “Dreams to Dream,” it provided a much-needed sense of comfort and solace.

The powerful combination of Linda Ronstadt’s angelic voice and the poignant lyrics allowed me to escape from my troubles and immerse myself in a world of dreams and possibilities. It reminded me that despite the hardships, there was still hope and that dreams had the power to transform my reality.

Furthermore, the atmospheric and sweeping orchestration of the song adds to its emotional impact. The delicate piano melodies, soaring strings, and subtle choir vocals create a sense of grandiosity and beauty that perfectly complements the lyrics.

The songwriting duo of James Horner and Will Jennings, who penned the lyrics and composed the music, deserve special recognition for creating such a poignant and timeless piece. Their collaboration resulted in a song that continues to resonate with audiences to this day.

In conclusion, Linda Ronstadt’s rendition of “Dreams to Dream (Finale Version)” captures the essence of the power of dreams. The song’s heartfelt lyrics, beautiful melody, and evocative orchestration combine to create a moving and inspiring experience. It is a reminder to hold onto our dreams, to never let go, and to always believe in their ability to come true.
