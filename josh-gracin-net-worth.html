Josh Gracin Net Worth: $2 Million

Josh Gracin is a country music singer from Westland, Michigan. He gained fame as a contestant on the second season of American Idol, where he finished fourth. Gracin started performing at a young age and won a national competition as a high school sophomore. He served in the United States Marine Corps as a supply clerk and was unable to participate in the American Idol Finalists tour due to his military commitment.

Gracin’s self-titled debut album was released in 2004 and was certified Gold. It contained the #1 US Country single “Nothin’ to Lose” and popular songs like “I Want to Live” and “Stay with Me (Brass Bed)”. His second album, We Weren’t Crazy, was released in 2008 and hit #4 on the US Country charts. His third album, Redemption, was released in 2011 and hit #39 on the US Country and US Indie charts.

Gracin married Ann Marie in 2001 and the couple has three daughters. In 2014, Gracin posted a suicide note on Facebook and was placed in a psychiatric hold. He later checked himself into an inpatient psychiatric facility.
