Elaine Bilstad began her acting career in 1988 with an appearance in the TV movie "What Price Victory" as Norleen. She appeared on one episode of "They Came from Out Space" as Julia Clearwater in 1990 and wasn't seen again on screen until she landed the part of Catherine in the TV movie "The Broken Chain" in 1993. Two years later, Bilstad played White Snow in "Happily Ever After: Fairy Tales for Every Child", and she landed the part of Shana Iron Shirt in one episode of the popular series "Walker, Texas Ranger" in 1996. She had a brief role in "Baywatch Nights" in 1997 and later in "Baywatch" as Anna the same year. Bilstad's last role came as the voice of Cholena in 1998's "An American Tail: The Treasure of Manhattan Island", which went straight to video. She also appeared as herself in the TV movie documentary "On Trial" in 1994 with Erick Avari, F. Lee Bailey, and Don Bloomfield.
