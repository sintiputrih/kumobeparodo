Who is Donny Schatz:

Donny Schatz is a famous Race Car Driver. He was born on August 10, 1977 and his birthplace is Minot, ND. Donny is also well known as, Sprint car driver known as a multiple-time Knoxville Nationals and World of Outlaws champion. He was the World of Outlaws Rookie of the Year award winner in 1997.

Donny is originated from United States. He and Steve Kinser are both World of Outlaws champions.
