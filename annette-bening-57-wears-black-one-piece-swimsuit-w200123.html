Annette Bening, 57, Wears Black One-Piece Swimsuit

Just like the little black dress, the black one-piece swimsuit will never go out of style! Case in point: Annette Bening looked like a total knockout rocking the ultra-flattering, scoop-neck style on the beach in Hawaii on Wednesday, March 23.

PHOTOS: Hot Celebs Over 40 in Swimsuits

Channeling her inner ’60s screen siren, the Tony-winning actress, 57, accessorized the match-with-anything swimwear with a ribbon-accented straw hat, a draped gray cover-up and round sunglasses.

Bening was on a spring break vacation with one of her daughters. She and her husband of 24 years, Warren Beatty, share four children: Stephen, 24, Benjamin, 21, Isabel, 19, and Ella, 15.

It was surely a welcome break for the American Beauty actress. She hasn’t been seen on a red carpet since she attended the National Urban Technology Center awards dinner in June 2015, but she’s been hard at work on three movies slated for 2016: The Seagull, 20th Century Women and a currently untitled project directed by Beatty.
