Kim Kardashian and Kanye West Show Off Sexy Ranch Photo Op

  • Kim Kardashian West shared more high-fashion photos from her recent vacation to her family's Wyoming ranch to Instagram on Sunday.
  • Both she and her husband, Kanye West, posed donning custom outfits from Nigerian-British designer Mowalola Ogunlesi.
  • Ogunlesi is set to be the design director for West's upcoming Yeezy collaboration with Gap, debuting in 2021.

Kim Kardashian and Kanye West are continuing their high-glam PDA streak.

On Sunday, Kim took to Instagram to share two additional photos from her recent Wyoming family vacation. In the new photos, we get a closer look at the couple's coordinating head-to-toe leather looks, which we saw in Kim's original Father's Day post from earlier this month.

For the photo op, Kim showed off her oxblood-hued, cutout jumpsuit, which she paired with a braided high ponytail and sleek rectangular sunglasses, while West wore an all-black vinyl leather ensemble paired with a neon-green T-shirt. Both looks were designed by Nigerian-British designer Mowalola Ogunlesi, who coincidentally enough will be the design director for West's upcoming Yeezy–Gap collaboration.

The announcement of West's multiyear deal with the staple mall brand was announced last week. According to an official press release, "Yeezy Gap" will be a collection of "modern, elevated basics for men, women and kids at accessible price points" while still retaining West's signature "design vision."

In addition to celebrating the announcement with a rare return to Twitter, West got to work revamping a flagship Gap store in downtown Chicago ahead of the upcoming collaboration.
