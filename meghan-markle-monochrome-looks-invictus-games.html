Meghan Markle Wears Three Monochrome Looks at the Invictus Games

Duchess Meghan has been embracing chic and easy nine-to-five dressing while at the Invictus Games with Prince Harry this week.

The royal couple were spotted cheering on athletes at the competition at Merkur Spiel-Arena in Düsseldorf, Germany, yesterday and earlier today. They both appeared relaxed in elegant yet laid-back ensembles, with Meghan opting for three different monochrome looks.

Today, to attend a meeting with NATO representatives on the sidelines of the sixth Invictus Games, the duchess styled a silky dark brown button-down top from L'Agence with light brown pants cinched with a built-in belt. She finished the look with brown suede pumps by Manolo Blahnik, gold hoop earrings, and black-tinted sunglasses with golden frames. Her hair was down in silky waves, and she wore minimal, rosy makeup.

Harry, meanwhile, went for his usual blue suit, white dress shirt, and black shoes—but he left his tie at home this time and unbuttoned his shirt at the top.

The Sussexes held hands as they walked on a running track.

picture alliance//Getty Images

The royals later changed into more casual looks to watch the sitting volleyball competition. Meghan looked lovely in a preppy white cable-knit sweater vest by Ralph Lauren, plus white skinny jeans and black pointed-toe flats with a strap on the ankle. She wore her gold Cartier watch and other gold bracelets and jewelry.

Harry stayed cool in a light blue button-down, black pants, and a black belt with a golden buckle.

Jordan Pettitt - PA Images//Getty Images

Yesterday, day four of the games, Meghan and Harry attended the wheelchair basketball final between the United States and France. The couple looked excited as they were photographed at the stadium in matching all-black looks.

Meghan wore a sleeveless scoop-neck tank with cropped skinny jeans and suede heels. She accessorized only with dainty gold and diamond jewelry, plus her beaded Invictus bracelet, and wore minimal makeup and a straight hairdo. Harry, next to her, wore an Invictus Games button-down, which he rolled up at the sleeves, straight-leg jeans, a black leather belt with a golden buckle, and white sneakers. He, too, accessorized with a gold bracelet and an Invictus beaded bracelet.
