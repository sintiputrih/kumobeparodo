Kathleen McCrone is an American lawyer. She is best known as being a wife of a famous singer and entertainer Wayne Newton. 

Married Life

She is second wife of her husband. She met with Wayne for the first time in his performance in 1990. 

After the first conversation, the duo fell in love with each other. 

The couple got married on 9 April, 1994 after having love affairs for several years. 

They invited their family and close friends on their wedding day.

Husband, Super Successful

Wayne Newton’s full name Carson Wayne Newton came to prominence for his song ‘Daddy, Don’t You Walk Fast’ in 1972. 

That song reached number 4 in Billboard Chart and sold over one million albums all over the world. 

He was born on 3 April, 1942 in Norfolk, Virginia, United States. His father, Patrick Newton, was an auto mechanic, and his mother’s name is Evelyn Marie. 

His family moved to Newark, Ohio, when he was a child. 

He began singing when he was a young age with his older brother. He began to learn the guitar and piano at the age of six.

He is one of the best entertainers of Las Vegas. He has several nicknames, such as The Midnight Idol, Mr. Las Vegas, and others. 

He has two children from his two marriages.
