Ben Kennedy Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Mr. Ben Kennedy (born 14 March 1974) is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer who played in the 1990s and 2000s. He was born in Casino, New South Wales, and is of Irish descent. His club football experience included stints with the Canberra Raiders, Newcastle Knights (with whom he won the 2001 NRL Premiership), and the Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles. He was a New South Wales State of Origin and Australian international representative forward.
