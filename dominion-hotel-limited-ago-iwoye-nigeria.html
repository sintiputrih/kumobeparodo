Dominion Hotel Limited, Ago Iwoye, Nigeria

A tastefully furnished hotel, situated away from the noisy metropolis, Dominion Hotel is an exclusive 3 Star Hotel with a unique blend of African Hospitality and a choice of exotic rooms that are second to none. Each room in Dominion Hotel is uniquely different in character and ambience. If you are looking forward to a rewarding vacation or a short period of recovery to regain balance, then the Dominion Hotel is just the right place for you. Our facilities include a small private pool, a sports bar, a night club, tastefully furnished rooms with 24hrs Live cable TV, 24 hours Wifi Internet Access and a restaurant serving an array of local and international cuisine.
