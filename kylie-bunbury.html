When They See Us actress Kylie Banbury Bio, Husband, Net Worth.

Kylie Bunbury is a Canadian/American fashion, lifestyle, model who later went on to pursue a career in the film industry as well. Through the incredible acting skills, Bunbury has managed to receive wide fans and followers. 

The beautiful and hardworking Kylie is better identified for her leading role as Genevieve "Ginny" Baker in the American drama series Pitch where an actor Mark Consuelos has also worked. 

Short Wiki

The Pitch actress Kylie was born in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada, on January 30, 1989. She is the only daughter of her parents Kristi Novak and Alex Bunbury. Kylie holds American/Canadian nationality and belongs to the mixed ethnic group. 

Kylie's father Alex who is from Guyana is a retired professional soccer player who played as a striker. Alex played for the Portuguese sports club Maritimo. 

The actress has two brothers Logan and Teal Banbury. Like, father Alex, Teal is also a professional football player who currently plays for New England Revolution as a midfielder. 

Got Engaged With Boyfriend

Not only the happiest professional life, but Kylie is also living a blissful love life. The 31-years-old actress Kylie is in a marital relationship with Jon-Ryan Alan Riggins. 

Kylie's beloved husband Jon-Ryan is the co-founder and COO of the bottled water company named Liquid Death Mountain Water. Jon who graduated from the University of Pittsburgh previously co-founded KOENA Inc and Saint Arbor LLC.

How and when Riggins and Banbury met is unclear. But it seems like the duo dated several months before they got engaged on April 8, 2018. The lovebird revealed the news of their engagement via their Instagram post. 

Married On January 1, 2020

After one-year-nine-months, Kylie and Jon-Ryan decided to share their wedding vows on January 1, 2020. The duo tied the wedding knot merrily in Funchal, Madeira. 

On the wedding day, Kylie was looking gorgeous in a V-neck long-sleeved embroidered wedding gown. Similarly, Jon-Ryan wore a blue suit-pant, matching waistcoat, a white shirt, and a black-tie. 

As of now, the newly married pair is sharing a good bond as a perfect husband and wife. They are spending their quality time together. Additionally, they have not planned any kids yet. 

Career Information

As mentioned above, before working as an actress, Kylie worked as a model. She has modeled for the bands such as Vivienne Westwood. Similarly, she has featured in Who What Wear and Essence Magazine.

Kylie began working as an actress back in 2010. Her first role was as Kathleen in the 2010 daytime soap opera Days of Our Lives. Then her first movie role was as Jordan Lundley in Prom directed by Joe Nussbaum.

Till now, Banbury has featured in movies and TV shows such as The Sitter, Game Night, Twisted, Under the Dome, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, The Twilight Zone, When They See Us, Brave New World, Big Sky, etc.
