Meredith Lepore - InStyle

Title: Contributing Writer

Education: Syracuse University

Location: New York, New York

Expertise: Fashion, Celebrity, Pop Culture

  • Meredith Lepore is a writer and editor based in New York who covers all things fashion, celebrity, and pop culture.
  • Her work has been published in The Observer, Travel + Leisure, Refinery29, Bustle, InStyle, Marie Claire, Self, Women's Health, and Cosmopolitan.
  • She has interviewed many celebrities, including Arianna Huffington, Sheryl Sandberg, Tory Burch, Priyanka Chopra, Venus Williams, Whitney Wolfe, Sara Blakely, Olivia Munn, Mark Duplass, and Lupita Nyong’o.

Experience

Meredith has been a writer and editor for over a decade, with her writing appearing online in The Observer, Travel + Leisure, Refinery29, Bustle, InStyle.com, and in magazines including Marie Claire, Self, Women's Health, InStyle, and Cosmopolitan. Her work has also been cited in The New York Times and New York Magazine. Her experience includes deputy editor at Ladders, editor-in-chief of the women's career site The Grindstone, and the director of content at Levo, a jobs networking platform for Millennial women.

Education

Meredith earned her Master's in Journalism from the Newhouse School at Syracuse University.
