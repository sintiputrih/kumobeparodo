The Lucha Bros Win ROH Tag Team Titles During Supercard Of Honor

Ring of Honor held a Reach for the Sky ladder match to determine the new world tag team champions at Supercard of Honor tonight. Now, we know who walked away from the match as the new champions.

Tony Khan created the match to honor Jay Briscoe after his untimely passing. It was previously reported that the winners of the match will receive a brand new set of tag team championships.

The Reach for the Sky match-up featured The Lucha Brothers (Rey Fenix and Penta), Top Flight (Dante Martine and Darius Martin), RUSH & Dralistico, Aussie Open, and The Kingdom.

The match contained plenty of breath-taking spots as all the competitors gave their everything to entertain the crowd. Dante Martin suffered nasty injury during the match. In the end, Rey Fenix climbed up the ladder to unhook the titles

The Lucha Bros. walked to the ramp where they were congratulated by former ROH World Tag Team Champions Mark Briscoe. Mark lost to Samoa Joe in a match for the ROH World Television Championship earlier during the show.
