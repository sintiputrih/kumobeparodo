linksgroupie - Trivia, Family, Bio

TikTok star and gamer who rose to fame on her linksgroupie account, where she posts POV-style videos and lip sync videos usually set to pop punk and emo songs. She has accumulated a total of 1.1 million followers and 19 million likes on the platform.

Before Fame

She made her TikTok debut in May 2020.

Trivia

She is also active on her linksgroupie Instagram account, where she shares selfies and photos of her outfits. She has amassed over 200,000 followers.

Family Life

She has a sister named Baylee and a cat named Loki.

Associated With

In December 2022, she posted a TikTok set to a clip from Bo Burnham's "Inside."
