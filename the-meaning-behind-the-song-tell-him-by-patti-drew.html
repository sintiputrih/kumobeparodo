The Meaning Behind The Song: Tell him by Patti Drew

In this article, we will explore the meaning behind the soulful and heartfelt song “Tell him” by Patti Drew. Released in 2013 as part of the soundtrack for Lee Daniels’ The Butler, this song captures the emotions of love, longing, and regret. Through its poignant lyrics, Patti Drew beautifully conveys the complex feelings that arise in a relationship that has faced challenges and temporarily lost its way.

Now let’s delve into the meaning behind the heartfelt lyrics of “Tell him.” The song begins with a plea to anyone who may come across the boy who holds the singer’s heart. She urges them to convey her message of love and care, emphasizing her willingness to be there for him whenever he may need her. The verse paints a picture of a recent argument that escalated, leading the boy to storm out and leave. However, the lyrics reveal that she didn’t mean the hurtful words she uttered, and she longs for him to come back to where he belongs.

The chorus of the song intensifies the depth of her emotions. Patti Drew repeats the plea to tell him how much she wants him and needs him. These simple words carry a profound longing and desire to mend the cracks that have appeared in their relationship, showcasing the vulnerability and yearning she feels.

As listeners, we can feel the raw emotions coursing through the lyrics and the soulful delivery of Patti Drew’s voice. The song captures the universal experience of love, and the challenges that can sometimes test its strength. It speaks to the desire to rectify mistakes and restore the connection between two individuals who share a history.

Personally, “Tell him” resonates with me on a deep level. As someone who has experienced the ups and downs of relationships, I can relate to the rollercoaster of emotions portrayed in this song. It reminds me of the times when conflicts escalated, leading to heated arguments and temporary separations. In those moments, when the dust settled, the regret and longing would wash over me, just like the lyrics of the song.

Music has a powerful way of evoking memories and emotions. When I listen to “Tell him,” it transports me back to those vulnerable moments when I realized the importance of expressing love and forgiveness. It serves as a reminder of the fragility of relationships and the need to nurture them with empathy and understanding.

The beauty of music lies in its ability to touch the hearts of listeners in unique ways. “Tell him” by Patti Drew is a timeless song that captures the essence of love, regret, and the yearning for reconciliation. Its soulful melody and meaningful lyrics continue to resonate with audiences, reminding us of the profound emotions that come with the territory of human relationships.

So, the next time you find yourself caught up in a moment of conflict with your loved one, take a pause and listen to “Tell him.” Let its heartfelt plea for forgiveness and reunion guide you towards finding common ground and rekindling the flame of love.

Remember, as Patti Drew beautifully croons, “Tell him to forgive me, tell him don’t be mean. I didn’t mean to start it, I wish it was all just a dream.”
