Nick Cannon Reacts to Elon Musk Secretly Welcoming Twins with Tesla Exec Shivon Zilis

Nick Cannon and Elon Musk have the same goal in mind.

On Wednesday (July 6), it was reported that the 51-year-old Tesla CEO had secretly welcomed twins with one of his top executives, Shivon Zilis, in November 2021, weeks before he and ex Grimes welcomed a daughter via surrogate in December. He is now a father of 10 kids.

The following day, Elon seemingly confirmed the news, tweeting, “Doing my best to help the underpopulation crisis. A collapsing birth rate is the biggest danger civilization faces by far.”

He then shared a follow-up tweet, writing, “Mark my words, they are sadly true” before adding, “I hope you have big families and congrats to those who already do!”

Elon‘s tweet caught the attention of Nick, 41, who is about to welcome his eighth child.

After seeing Elon‘s tweet about the “collapsing birth rate,” Nick tweeted to Elon, “Right there with you my Brother!✊?”

Elon shares shares 18-year-old twins Vivian Jenna Wilson and Griffin Musk, along with 16-year-old triplets Kai, Damian, and Sax Musk, with his first wife, Justine Wilson. The two had another son, Nevada, who died at 10 weeks.

He and Grimes share 2-year-old son X Æ A-XII and seven-month-old daughter Exa Dark Sideræl.

Nick currently has seven kids with four different women – 11-year-old twins Monroe and Moroccan with ex-wife Mariah Carey, 5-year-old Golden “Sagon” and 1-year-old Powerful Queen with Brittany Bell, 1-year-old twins Zion and Zillion with Abby De La Rosa, and Zen – who sadly died at 5-months-old in December 2021 – with Alyssa Scott.

Earlier this year, Nick confirmed that he is expecting his eighth child with Bre Tiesi.

In a recent interview, Nick confirmed that he has even more kids on the way this year.
