Pics Of The Action Film Actor Hollywood Life

Scott Adkins went to the movie premiere for ‘Doctor Strange’ on Oct. 24, 2016 at the Westminster Abbey in London.

He was born on June 17, 1976, making him currently 42 years old. He expressed interest in martial arts as a child, and practiced Taekwondo and kickboxing. Scott went on to his first film role, ‘Dei Seung Chui Keung,’ in 2001. He has since appeared in ‘Doctor Strange,’ ‘The Bourne Ultimatum,’ ‘The Expendables 2,’ and ‘Zero Dark Thirty.’ Scott’s latest role is in ‘Avengement,’ in which he portrays a killer seeking revenge on people who ‘made him a killer.’ The actor had to wear fake teeth and was put in the makeup chair around three or four times a day, he told HollywoodLife EXCLUSIVELY ahead of the film’s premiere on May 24.
