What To Wear To The Hamptons: Dresses, Jumpsuits, Hats

Nothing says modern American luxury quite like a long weekend in the Hamptons. Celebs including Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez, Scarlett Johansson and Colin Jost, Scott Disick, and all of the stars of The Real Housewives of New York City are spotted every summer enjoying a quiet weekend in one of the cute and sleepy towns on the coast of Long Island.

Of course, not everyone has the luxury of heading to the Hamptons for Memorial Day weekend, but wherever you are you can still look like you’re relaxing by the pool at a fancy summer estate. We like all-American preppiness for this look — think stripes, crisp lines and chic sunnies. You’ll also need an on-trend and convenient beach bag, some easy-breezy dresses and of course, a wide-brimmed summer hat never goes amiss.
